Cook It Up

My final project for my Android Studio class. It's an app which allows the user to input whatever ingredients they want. Utilizing an API with a plethora of recipes, the app pulls recipes which use the inputted ingredients and presents them to the user. In addition, it lets the user know if more ingredients are required to prepare the presented dish as well as links which lead to more in-depth instructions on creating the dish.

2D/3D Maze

Using JFrame in Java, I created a maze with Pac Man themed elements which the player could navigate through. In addition to the creation of a 2D playable maze, I created a side by side 3D representation of the current state of the game shown through the players POV. There are also extra's added into the game where the player must find and obtain these items in order to unlock the exit to the maze.

Term-Deposit-ML-Model

A ML Logistic Regression Model to determine which customers are most likely to deposit cash in a term deposit with a certain bank after a telephonic marketing campaign

2D Side-Scroller

Utilizing this prefab set my partner and I created a 2D side scroller RPG in Unity. The player has the ability to attack enemies and also receives damage from incoming enemy attacks. Using rigid body's and colliders, we also implemented physics for when the player or enemy runs out of HP. The body gets broken apart and the individual parts fall and collide with one another (it's not as brutal as it sounds, I promise).

Marine Conservation

Traveled to Bayahibe, Dominican Republic as an EF Tour volunteer to learn about marine conservation. As a volunteer group, we learned about coral reef bleaching and the effects it has on the enviroment and the economy. We learened from and worked with Marine biologists from Fun DeMar, an organization dedicaated to preserving the coral reefs. We also took part in mapping the local reefs, cleaning local beaches, data collection of harmful trash, and buoy construction.
