Books

Currently reading Can't Hurt Me: Master your Mind and Defy the Odds by David Goggins. It's an autobiography where Goggins shares his life story and the obstacles he's had to overcome in order to achieve his dreams.

The School of Greatness Kobe Bryant

This interview of Kobe Bryant on The School of Greatness podcast is one of my favorites to listen to. As he talks about what he calls the Mamba Mentality, which is essentially is a mindset for constant self-improvement through hard work. It inspires me to always put my best effort into anything I do despite what obstacles or challenges I face.

"Everything negative—pressure, challenges—is all an opportunity for me to rise." - Kobe Bryant
